Goulash

Description

Half Ground Beef Half Macoroni for the weightlifters.

Ingridents

  • 3 lbs of Ground Beef
  • 3 cups of Macoroni
  • 1 can of tomato sauce, tomatoes, tomato paste each
  • Sprinkle of salt and pepper
  • 4 tlb of italian seasoning
  • 6 tlb of worchester sauce
  • 1 whole onion
  • 6 cloves of garlic

Steps

  1. chop onions
  2. cook ground beef and onion until no longer pink
  3. drain fat
  4. add chopped garlic,, salt, pepper, italian seasoning, worchester, tomato sauce, tomatoes, tomato paste
  5. once boiled, bring heat to very low and put a lop on it and let it simmer for half an hour.
  6. remove from heat
  7. boil 4quartz of water to a running boil
  8. add macoroni
  9. stir frequently for 5 minutes
  10. drain pasta
  11. add half macoroni and the stew mixture for goulash
  12. enjoy!
