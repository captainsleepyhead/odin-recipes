Scrambled Eggs

Description

A yellow-ish delight.

Ingridents

  • 5 large eggs
  • 1 tlb olive oil
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. pour a tlb of olive oil into a pan
  2. once hot, break 5 eggs into the pan and stir as the eggs coagulate, remove eggs once most of the wetness has dried.
  3. sprikle salt and pepper to taste
